Resume Building

Tips to follow to create an impressive resume:

1. Customize: Tailor your resume to the specific job by aligning your skills and experience with the job description.
2. Keywords: Use relevant keywords from the job description to enhance ATS visibility.
3. Quantify Achievements: Use metrics to showcase your impact, such as percentage increases or project outcomes.
4. Strong Verbs: Employ strong action verbs, avoiding clich√©s, to describe your accomplishments.
5. Proofread: Carefully review your resume for typos and errors to maintain professionalism.
6. Technical Skills: Highlight your technical expertise, including programming languages and tools.
7. Project Highlights: Include notable projects and contributions to demonstrate real-world experience.
8. Specific Examples: Be specific and provide examples to illustrate your qualifications.
9. Tailor Again: Customize your resume for the specific role you're applying for.
10. Seek Feedback: Have a trusted friend or colleague review your resume for improvements.
